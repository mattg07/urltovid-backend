To influence people, make one key change in how you talk. Asking questions can actually help you garner influence and change minds. Communication expert Matt Abrahams states that asking a question puts you in a position of power, demonstrating care and empathy. Good questions are concise, build on previous conversation, and focus on the topic. Practice asking questions thoughtfully, listen actively, and aim to foster collaboration. This approach can strengthen relationships and enhance your influence in various settings.
